ABOUT

I am a PhD student in Architecture with Buildings-Landscapes-Cultures concentration and a distinguished graduate student fellow at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, and also a Fulbright scholar from Japan. Prior to serving the current positons, I obtained BEng in Urban Engineering from the University of Tokyo, and MArch in Urban Design from the University College London.

My current research interest is in the intesection of body, sense, and everyday life in urban public spaces, especially from the perspectives of performance and practice. Coming from an engineering school and minoring in folklore and environmental history, my ambition is to cross disciplinary borders to fully comprehend everyday phenomena.
